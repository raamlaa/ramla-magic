### 🎨 What I Do

As **Packaging Strategist**, I create concept-driven thumbnails that blend scientific accuracy with curiosity hooks:

- **Visual storytelling**: Using expressions, annotated scans, and medical imagery
- **Bold messaging**: Text overlays like "Flawed Study?" or "Is REMS legit?" that spark curiosity
- **CTR optimization**: Designing thumbnails that balance professionalism with click-worthiness
- **Trust-driven design**: Maintaining credibility while maximizing engagement

---

### 🧠 The Strategy

Every thumbnail is designed to:

- **Hook curiosity** without sensationalism
- **Communicate value** at a glance
- **Build trust** through professional execution
- **Drive clicks** while respecting the audience's intelligence

My goal is to make complex medical content accessible and engaging, turning evidence into impact.
